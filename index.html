About Admissions

The Admissions Office of Colegio San Agustin Makati is the welcoming gateway for students and families seeking quality Catholic education. We are committed to guiding applicants through a smooth and transparent admissions process, beginning with online application and document submission, followed by entrance testing and evaluation. Our team ensures each applicant is fairly assessed based on academic performance, conduct, and alignment with Augustinian values. We support families at every stage, from inquiry to enrollment, promoting a nurturing and faith-driven environment where students grow intellectually, morally, and spiritually. Admissions Policy

As a Catholic School+

As a Catholic school, CSA holds strongly to the Catholic faith and its practices, which must be complied with. Students and parents are expected to actively participate or express full support for the values espoused by the school.

As a School for Student Leaders+

CSA fosters leadership qualities in students, encouraging them to take initiative and demonstrate responsibility. The school provides opportunities for students to develop leadership skills through various programs and activities.

As a Basic Education and Accredited School+

CSA is managed in accordance with government policies and conforms to national and international standards. Per DepEd Order No. 47, series of 2016, the school implements a cut-off age for incoming Nursery, Junior Kinder, Senior Kinder, and Grade 1 applicants. For Grade 11 STEM strand, per DepEd Order No. 55, series of 2016, a minimum final grade of 85 in both Science and Math in Grade 10 is required, along with a percentile rank of 86 or above in the STEM subtest of the career assessment exam.

Admission to CSA Makati is Conditional+

Admission to CSA Makati is conditional. The school reserves the right to refuse acceptance or re-admission to any student whose behavior or academic standing does not meet the school's standards. Students with special needs may apply, provided an official certification from a specialist detailing specific needs and interventions is submitted for proper accommodation.
